Our Journey

NextGen was founded in 2019 with a mission to make quality technical education accessible to everyone in Nepal and beyond. What started as a small initiative to bridge the gap between traditional education and industry requirements has grown into a comprehensive learning platform.

From our humble beginnings with just a handful of courses, we've evolved into a platform serving over 50,000 students worldwide. Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, student success stories, and a relentless commitment to educational excellence.
